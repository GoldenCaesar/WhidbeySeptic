Our Story

From Service to Service

Whidbey Septic is a family-owned business operated by brothers, Ben and Seth. Both veterans, they bring the same dedication, discipline, and attention to detail to septic services that they honed during their military careers. Raised on Whidbey Island, they understand the unique needs of the community and are committed to providing reliable, high-quality service.

Our Commitment to Whidbey Island

We believe in honesty, integrity, and hard work. Our goal is to not just meet, but exceed your expectations. We treat every customer like family, ensuring your septic system is in top condition. We're proud to serve our neighbors and contribute to the well-being of Whidbey Island.
